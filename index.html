Rules Of The Game

Here's a summary of the winning combinations:

  1. Rock wins against Scissors.
  2. Scissors win against Paper.
  3. Paper wins against Rock.
  • The objective of the game is to choose the winning hand gesture and defeat your opponent's choice.

  • It's a game of chance and strategy, try to anticipate your opponent's move and choose the gesture that defeats it
